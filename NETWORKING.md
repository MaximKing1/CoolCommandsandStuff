# Networking

## Interfaces:
* **wlan0** = Wireless
* **wlo1** = Wired (Ethernet)

## IPv4 VS IPv6
* **IPv4** = EXAMPLE: 192.168.0.1
* **IPv6** = EXAMPLE: 2001:0db8:85a3:0000:0000:8a2e:0370:7334

## Mac Address
* The MAC Address is the psyical address of the device
* use `ifconfig` to get your MAC Address
